About the Project

Rivaldo believes that every frame carries a story. His work blends creativity with precision — turning raw moments into compelling visual narratives. Whether it's a brand campaign, music video, or documentary-style content, he brings emotion and purpose to the screen.

With a strong foundation in videography and editing, Rivaldo approaches each project with a unique artistic vision. From planning the perfect shot to fine-tuning edits in post-production, he ensures that every detail aligns with the message and mood of the story.
