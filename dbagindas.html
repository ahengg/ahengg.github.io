Drone Pilot – Dbagindas x Ipang

Indonesia Selamanya (Official Music Video)

Project Overview

In the official music video “Indonesia Selamanya” by Dbagindas x Ipang, Rivaldo contributed as a freelance drone pilot, capturing dynamic aerial footage that elevated the emotional impact of the video.

His shots showcased sweeping views of the landscape, reinforcing the song’s powerful message of unity and national pride. From high skies above open fields to fly-throughs of cultural locations, every scene added cinematic depth to the music.

Featured Drone Scenes

Aerial footage by Rivaldo for “Indonesia Selamanya”

Working closely with the video director and production crew, Rivaldo ensured that every drone sequence matched the pacing and emotional arc of the song. He contributed from location scouting to executing complex aerial maneuvers.

This project highlights Rivaldo’s growing portfolio as a drone videographer — blending storytelling, atmosphere, and national spirit from the sky.
