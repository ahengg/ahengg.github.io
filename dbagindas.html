Dbagindas x Ipang – Indonesia Selamanya

In the official music video “Indonesia Selamanya” by Dbagindas x Ipang, Rivaldo contributed as a freelance drone pilot, capturing dynamic aerial footage that elevated the emotional impact of the video. His shots showcased sweeping views of the landscape, reinforcing the song’s powerful message of unity and national pride.

From the high skies above open fields to precise fly-throughs of cultural locations, Rivaldo’s drone operation helped set the cinematic tone for the music video. His eye for framing and movement brought elegance and drama to every aerial scene.

Working closely with the video director and production crew, Rivaldo ensured that every drone sequence matched the pacing and emotional arc of the song. His role extended from location scouting and planning flight paths to executing complex aerial maneuvers.

This project highlights Rivaldo’s growing portfolio as a drone videographer — blending storytelling, emotion, and visual direction from the skies.
