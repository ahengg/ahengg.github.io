About the Project

In the official event video “PSRI – Pocari Sweat Run 2024”, Rivaldo contributed as a freelance drone pilot, capturing dynamic aerial footage that highlighted the energy, determination, and scale of the run.

From sweeping overhead shots of the race to immersive fly-throughs of cheering crowds and urban scenery, the visuals brought storytelling and emotion together — blending technical mastery with artistic vision.
