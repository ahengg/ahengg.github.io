Creating Visuals that Connect

Rivaldo is a visual storyteller with professional experience in videography and photography. He has worked with notable brands such as Garmin, HOKKA, Under Armour, Ortuseight, and multiple running event organizers across Indonesia.

His portfolio includes branded campaigns, product shoots, athlete profiles, and immersive event coverage — all crafted to capture emotion, movement, and identity through the lens.

This website is a curated showcase of Rivaldo’s creative journey. Whether you're planning a brand campaign or a large-scale event, Rivaldo brings a sharp eye for detail and a deep understanding of visual storytelling to every project.

All content is original and created with artistic intent — please reach out for collaboration or permission to use any featured works.
