The Secret of Our House (English Subtitle) – Trailer

*The Secret of Our House* is a psychological short film exploring family tension, hidden trauma, and the silence that speaks louder than words. The trailer teases a slow-burn narrative, filled with mystery, emotional weight, and cinematic suspense.

Rivaldo served as both the videographer and editor for this trailer — crafting each frame with intention and subtle storytelling. His cinematography captures quiet but intense moments, while his editing builds a rhythmic tension that leaves viewers curious and unsettled.

Working closely with the director and production crew, Rivaldo shaped the trailer’s tone through pacing, music, and visual flow. His ability to balance subtle emotional beats with suspenseful storytelling shines through in every second of this preview.

*The Secret of Our House* is a testament to Rivaldo’s skills in both visual composition and narrative editing — showing how atmosphere can be built not just through story, but through the way it's seen and felt.
